Price Discrimination — Ценовая дискриминация

What is Price Discrimination? / Что такое ценовая дискриминация?

English: Charging different prices to different customers for the same good, based on willingness to pay. Requires market power and ability to segment customers.

Русский: Установление разных цен разным покупателям за один товар в зависимости от готовности платить.

Mark-up and Elasticity / Наценка и эластичность

(P - MC) / P = 1 / |ε|

Higher elasticity (more elastic demand) → lower mark-up. Lower elasticity → higher mark-up.

Three Degrees of Price Discrimination / Три степени ценовой дискриминации

Degree / СтепеньDescription / ОписаниеExamples / Примеры
FirstCharge each customer their max willingness to pay (perfect discrimination)Auctions, negotiation
SecondDifferent prices for different quantities (quantity discounts, versioning)Bulk discounts, software tiers
ThirdDifferent prices for different market segments (age, location, etc.)Student discounts, regional pricing

First Degree (Perfect) / Первая степень (совершенная)

Firm captures all consumer surplus. Each unit sold at buyer's reservation price.

Third Degree / Третья степень

Segment markets by elasticity. Charge higher price to less elastic segment (e.g., business travelers vs leisure).
