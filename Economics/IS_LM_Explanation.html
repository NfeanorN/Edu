IS-LM Model — Модель IS-LM

What is IS-LM? / Что такое IS-LM?

English: Model of goods market (IS) and money market (LM) together. Determines equilibrium Y and interest rate i simultaneously.

Русский: Модель рынка товаров (IS) и денежного рынка (LM). Определяет равновесные Y и i.

IS Curve (Goods Market) / Кривая IS (Рынок товаров)

IS = combinations of (Y, i) where goods market is in equilibrium (Z = Y).

Slope negative: Higher i → less investment → less demand → lower Y.

Y = C(Y) + I(i) + G — I depends negatively on i

i
  |
  | \
  |  \  IS (downward sloping)
  |   \
  |    \
  |
  0+----+----+----+----+ Y

Shifts: G↑ or T↓ → IS shifts right. G↓ or T↑ → IS shifts left.

LM Curve (Money Market) / Кривая LM (Денежный рынок)

LM = combinations of (Y, i) where money market is in equilibrium (M/P = L(Y,i)).

Slope positive: Higher Y → more money demand → higher i (for given M).

M/P = L(Y, i) — L rises with Y, falls with i

i
  |
  |     /
  |    /  LM (upward sloping)
  |   /
  |  /
  |
  0+----+----+----+----+ Y

Shifts: M↑ → LM shifts right. M↓ → LM shifts left.

Equilibrium / Равновесие

Intersection of IS and LM = simultaneous equilibrium in both markets.
